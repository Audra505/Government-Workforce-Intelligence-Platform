# Purpose

This document defines the complete user experience for the HR Director persona.

The objective is to provide:

- Strategic Workforce Visibility
- Decision Support
- Forecast Oversight
- Staffing Risk Awareness
- Approval Workflows

---

# Persona Summary

Role:

```text
HR Director
```

Primary Goal:

```text
Maintain Workforce Readiness
Optimize Staffing Levels
Reduce Organizational Risk
```
