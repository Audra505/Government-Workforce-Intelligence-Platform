# Purpose

This document defines the executive-level user experience for organizational leadership.

The objective is to provide:

- Strategic Visibility
- Workforce Intelligence
- Organizational Readiness Monitoring
- Risk Awareness
- Executive Decision Support

---

# Persona Summary

Role:

```text
Executive Leader
```

Primary Goal:

```text
Maintain Organizational Readiness
Reduce Workforce Risk
Support Strategic Decisions
```
